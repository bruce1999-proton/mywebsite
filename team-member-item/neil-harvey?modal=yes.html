Neil Harvey

Co-Founder & Director

Neil has spent (almost) his entire career in tech recruitment. The impersonal, transactional nature of recruitment never sat well with Neil and he knew there must be a more personal way to help connect tech talent with amazing careers.

So, in 2016 he took the plunge and set up Noa with that goal squarely in mind.

The success of Noa Recruitment has been down to the unwavering commitment to delivering an amazing experience to candidates and clients.

When Neil isn’t working you can finding him running or spending time with his amazing wife and two beautiful daughters.
